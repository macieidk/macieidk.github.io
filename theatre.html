theatre

i've participated in many shows, both on stage and backstage. i'm immensely grateful for the opportunities that have been bestowed upon me by the many directors i have had the privilege to work with. certainly, i wish to further my experience in the theatrical industry as i depart high school.

i began acting at age thirteen at a summer theatre camp at a nearby nature center. i didn't necessarily know what to expect; i only knew i wanted to be perform in beauty and the beast jr. and so, i gave it a whirl . . . and i've never regretted such a thing. i discovered my love for performing . . . i caught the theatrical bug . . . and i never wanted to let it go.

and so i continued.
